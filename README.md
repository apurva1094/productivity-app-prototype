# Productivity App Prototype

An open-source productivity project concept to help students manage tasks,
reminders, and schedules in a simple and privacy-friendly way.

## 🎯 Problem Statement
Students often juggle assignments, exams, and daily goals across multiple tools.
This leads to missed deadlines and poor organization.

## 💡 Proposed Solution
A lightweight productivity application that combines:
- Task management
- Reminders
- Simple scheduling
- Priority tagging
- Offline-friendly design

## 👥 Target Users
- College students
- School students
- Self-learners

## 🛣️ Roadmap (During Hackathon)
Week 1: Research & requirement analysis  
Week 2: UX mockups and flow design  
Week 3: Feature refinement & documentation  
Week 4: Final polish and submission
